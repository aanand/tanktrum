(An incomplete clone of) Death Tank!

God damn! Look at this! We've implemented, like, an amount of that classic Sega Saturn easter egg, Death Tank Zwei, in an online-multiplayer capacity!

Quick start!

Click here to play it right the hell now!

  • You'll need a Java - any old 1.5+ will do.
  • There should be a server running on norgg.org on the default port. If there isn't then find some friends and start your own, unless you're a terrible sociopath, in which case this expression is recommended: :(
  • You will be asked to accept a security certificate. We recommend that you do so, or you'll not be able to play it, and clicking on that link will have been a gigantic waste of your time.

Controls

A moves left.
D moves right.
Left and right arrows aim.
Up and down arrows control gun power.
Space fires.
Tab changes weapon.
Arrow keys and Enter control the menus.
T is for chat.

Okay, so.

We started work on this a few weeks before it was reported that a game called Death Tank appears to be coming to Xbox Live Arcade. If indeed this is the Death Tank of old times, then while that's great (the whole reason we started this thing is so we could play Death Tank online) it's admittedly somewhat dispiriting - damn them! We were doing that!

However, we can think of several reasons this is still worth people's time:

  • We're not charging money for it!
  • Hey, what if it's not even the Death Tank coming out on XBLA, but just some game that happens be called Death Tank, or - gasp - some kind of rubbishy sequel with none of the original's raw charm? Our game will be better! Better than an actual real game someone is charging money for!
  • You can play this on any PC or Mac or Linux machine, not just an Xbox 360! That means you can tell your mum about it on MSN and be blowing up a polygonal representation of a tank she's controlling, literally within seconds!
  • That port's probaby being churned out by a faceless corporation somewhere. Seriously, we bet it's called CloneCorp or Game Porting Solutions or some shit. They probably have to wear four ties to work. Whereas we are some extremely cool people. If you met us at a party it would be the best party you had been to in at least a week. No joke.

Puts it all in perspective, right? You definitely want to be seen playing the Death Tank clone written by the cool people.
